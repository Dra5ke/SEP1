The first turning of the wheel

Friday 14:00 PM - Sunday 16:00 PM

Study of the Buddha's teachings is an essential part of Buddhist practice, and the aim of this workshop is to acquaint students with some of the key texts of early Indian Buddhism. It is suitable for students of all traditions and focuses on the core teachings held in common by all Buddhist schools. The course will illustrate how a deeper knowledge of the scriptures can illuminate practice. The workshop will take 2 days, Friday and Sunday from 14:00 PM to 16:00 PM.
