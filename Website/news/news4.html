Essence of the Prajina Paramita Heart Sutra

The most popular scripture

The Heart Sūtra is a popular sutra in Mahāyāna Buddhism. Its Sanskrit title, Prajñāpāramitāhṛdaya, can be translated as "The Heart of the Perfection of Wisdom". The Heart Sūtra is often cited as the best-known and most popular Mahāyāna Buddhist scripture. The text has been translated into English dozens of times from both Chinese and Sanskrit.
