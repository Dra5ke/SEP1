Transformational Workshop
Friday 12:00 AM - Sunday 19:00 PM
Held by: Igor Igorovic
Price: 300 kr

Evening 1 – MINDSET RESET: Learn to meditate with Lis. Lis is a meditation, mindfulness and yoga expert. She has been influential in the wellness scene for over 7 years, facilitating holistic courses, retreats and workshops for health, creativity and performance. She is fluent at making the ancient practices of yoga and meditation relatable to modern living and believes they are invaluable life skills to stress less and access our most calm and fulfilled self.

Evening 2 – BACK TO NATURE: Get back in touch with nature with Mayanya. Nature teaches us that everything has a purpose and to always live in the moment. MaYanya is an herbalist, who over the past several years has developed a range of beautiful herbal harmonic tea blends. Her ability to hear and understand the energetic language which is the “voice of the planet” has developed a unique insight into the “body wisdom” interface between humans and Mother Earth. She teaches this body of work, utilizing the healing harmonies in the herbal blends as the platform to re-introduce people all over the world, to their own intuitive voice.

Evening 3 – EARTH TO TABLE: Learn about nutrition and our relationship to food with Jess. Jess began her understanding of food as medicine in 2003, when she commenced studies in Western Herbal Medicine and primitive nature-based skills. She has since learnt to focus on the unique needs of each person, through studies in Numerology, Bowen Therapy and Yoga. In 2008 Jess co-created, and currently owns and operates SkinDoona, a business in which she manufactures and distributes herbal skincare

This event will be held on Friday at 12:00 AM and it will conclude 2 days later on Sunday at 19:00 PM.
